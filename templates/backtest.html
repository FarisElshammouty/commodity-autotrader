What this does: Replays 1-hour historical candles through the live strategy logic. Entry/exit prices include realistic spread (bid/ask cost per symbol) and slippage (0.01% per side). Results show how the current parameters would have performed historically — not a guarantee of future results.

Walk-Forward Optimization: Splits history into a training window (70%) and an out-of-sample test window (30%). Searches 144 parameter combinations across signal threshold, ATR stop multiplier, risk/reward ratio, and risk per trade. The best combination from training is then validated on the unseen test data — which gives a realistic measure of whether the parameters are genuinely good or just overfit to the past.

Monte Carlo Drawdown Analysis: Shuffles your trade P&L sequence 1,000 times to build a statistical distribution of worst-case drawdowns. This shows the probability of ruin (hitting the $24,000 floor) and the expected drawdown at various confidence levels (90th, 95th, 99th percentile). Use this to understand tail risk — not just what happened, but what could happen with the same trades in a different order.
